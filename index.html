Chester Bennington (1976 - 2017)

Chester Charles Bennington was an American singer, best known as the lead vocalist of rock band Linkin Park. He was also a lead vocalist of Grey Daze, Dead by Sunrise and Stone Temple Pilots at various points.

Timeline

  • 1976: Born in Phoenix, Arizona
  • 1987: Parents divorced
  • 1993: Releases first cassete with Band Sean Dowdell and His Friends
  • 1993: Leaves Sean Dowdell and His Friends and forms Grey Daze
  • 1998: Leaves Grey Daze
  • 1999: Joins Linkin Park (then known as Xero)
  • 2000: Band releases debut album Hybrid Theory
  • 2005: Co-founds Dead by Sunrise
  • 2013: Gets recruited to replace Stone Temple Pilots' vocalist
  • 2017: Dies via suicide
